Summary of MLE

You can use MLE to answer these questions! In addition, maximum likelihood estimation is really easy - a three-step process! You just need to know some high school math. In general, this is what you do:

  • Write the likelihood function.
    • This is just the pdf or pmf over all your data!
  • Take the log of the likelihood function
    • This is called the log-likelihood function.
  • Set the derivative of the log-likelihood function equal to 0 and solve.
    • (for the parameter you are interested in).

Don't worry, I'll explain these 3 steps in detail.
